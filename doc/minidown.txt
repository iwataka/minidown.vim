*minidown.txt* Minimalist markdown preview

*Commands*                                *minidown-commands*

:Minidown [files]             Open the previews of given files in your primary
                              browser.

:Minidown                     Open the preview of the current buffer in your
                              primary browser.

*Requirements*                            *minidown-requirements*

You should install pandoc command and xdg-open if you use Linux. Also your Vim
must be compiled with +python or +ruby.
